Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our home relocation services.

How far in advance should I book my home relocation?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance, especially during peak moving seasons (summer months and weekends). However, we can often accommodate last-minute requests based on availability. For international moves, we suggest planning 6-8 weeks ahead.

What items require special handling?

Items that typically need special handling include pianos, antiques, artwork, fragile electronics, aquariums, plants, and hazardous materials. We provide specialized packing and transportation for these items with additional care and appropriate materials.

Can I pack some items myself?

Yes, you can pack some items yourself if you prefer. However, we recommend using our professional packing service to ensure all items are properly protected. If you do pack yourself, we can provide packing materials and guidance on proper techniques.

How are fragile items protected during the move?

Fragile items are packed with multiple layers of protection including bubble wrap, packing paper, and custom crating when needed. We use specialized boxes for dishes, glasses, and electronics. All fragile items are clearly marked and transported with extra care.

What happens if something gets damaged during the move?

In the rare event of damage, our insurance coverage will compensate you for the item's value. We have a straightforward claims process - simply report the damage within 7 days of delivery, provide documentation, and we'll work quickly to resolve the issue.

Do you provide storage solutions if needed?

Yes, we offer secure, climate-controlled storage facilities for both short-term and long-term needs. Whether you need to store items before moving into your new home or want to declutter, we can accommodate your storage requirements.
