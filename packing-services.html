Packing Services FAQs

Do you provide all packing materials?

Yes, we provide all necessary packing materials including boxes, bubble wrap, packing paper, tape, and specialty containers. All materials are included in our service packages.

How do you pack fragile items?

Fragile items receive special attention with individual wrapping, cushioned boxes, and clearly marked "fragile" labels. We use techniques like double-boxing for extremely delicate items.

Can I pack some items myself?

Absolutely! We offer partial packing services where you can pack some items yourself and we handle the rest. We can also provide materials for your DIY packing.

Do you offer unpacking services?

Yes, we provide unpacking services at your destination. We can unpack all items, place them in designated rooms, and remove all packing materials.

How long does packing take?

Packing time depends on the size of your home and number of items. A typical 3-bedroom home takes 6-8 hours, while smaller homes may take 3-4 hours.
