Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our logistics services

General Questions

What areas do you serve in Pakistan?

We provide nationwide coverage across all major cities in Pakistan including Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad, Rawalpindi, Faisalabad, Peshawar, Multan, Hyderabad, Quetta, and many more. Our network extends to over 50 cities and continues to grow to serve more locations.

How long has Jiffy Logistics been in business?

Jiffy Logistics was established in 2010 and has been providing reliable logistics solutions for over 10 years. We've grown from a small local operation to one of Pakistan's leading logistics providers with a fleet of over 150 vehicles and a team of 200+ professionals.

What industries do you specialize in serving?

We serve a wide range of industries including e-commerce, retail, pharmaceuticals, food & beverage, manufacturing, and more. Our specialized services like cold chain logistics make us particularly suited for temperature-sensitive products in the pharmaceutical and food sectors.

Are your services available 24/7?

Yes, our customer support and emergency services are available 24/7. While standard deliveries occur during business hours, we offer after-hours and weekend services for urgent shipments at an additional charge.

How do I know my shipment is secure with Jiffy Logistics?

We implement multiple security measures including GPS tracking, tamper-proof seals, and trained security personnel for high-value shipments. All our vehicles are equipped with security features, and we conduct thorough background checks on all employees.

What makes Jiffy Logistics different from other providers?

Our combination of technology, experience, and customer focus sets us apart. We offer real-time tracking, 99% on-time delivery rates, dedicated account managers, and customized logistics solutions tailored to each client's specific needs.

Pricing & Payments

How are shipping costs calculated?

Our pricing is based on several factors including package dimensions, weight, distance, service level (standard or express), and any special handling requirements. We offer transparent pricing with no hidden fees - you'll see the complete cost breakdown before confirming your shipment.

Do you offer volume discounts for regular shipments?

Yes, we provide significant discounts for high-volume shippers and contract customers. Our account managers can create customized pricing plans based on your shipping patterns, with discounts increasing with volume. Contact our sales team to discuss your specific needs.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash on delivery, bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and mobile payments. Business clients can also set up monthly billing accounts with 30-day terms upon credit approval. Online payments can be made through our secure portal.

Are there any additional fees I should know about?

All standard fees are included in your quote. Potential additional charges could include: residential delivery fees, liftgate service, inside delivery, storage fees if delivery is delayed, or special handling for oversized items. These will always be communicated upfront.

Do you offer price matching for competitors' rates?

We're confident in our competitive pricing, but will review competitor quotes on a case-by-case basis for comparable services. Price matching is available for contract customers shipping similar volumes with the same service levels and coverage.

Is insurance included in the shipping cost?

Basic coverage is included at no extra charge (up to a certain value per kg). Additional insurance can be purchased for high-value items. Our team can help you determine the appropriate coverage level for your shipment during the quoting process.

Our Services

What types of items can you transport?

We can transport most non-hazardous items including household goods, commercial products, machinery, documents, and temperature-sensitive items. Prohibited items include hazardous materials, illegal goods, live animals, and certain restricted items. Contact us if you're unsure about a specific item.

Do you offer packing services?

Yes, we provide professional packing services using high-quality materials to ensure your items are protected during transit. Our team is trained in proper packing techniques for fragile items, electronics, and specialty goods. We can pack entire shipments or just specific items as needed.

What is your cold chain logistics service?

Our cold chain service maintains temperature control for perishable goods throughout the entire supply chain. We use refrigerated vehicles, temperature monitoring devices, and specialized handling procedures for pharmaceuticals, food products, and other temperature-sensitive items.

Can you handle oversized or heavy shipments?

Yes, we have equipment and expertise to handle oversized, heavy, or awkwardly shaped items. Our fleet includes flatbed trucks, liftgates, and other specialized equipment. For extremely large shipments, we can arrange cranes or other heavy lifting equipment as needed.

Do you provide warehousing services?

Yes, we offer secure warehousing solutions in multiple locations across Pakistan. Our facilities include climate-controlled storage, inventory management systems, and can handle everything from short-term storage to long-term inventory management with fulfillment services.

What e-commerce fulfillment services do you offer?

Our e-commerce fulfillment services include inventory storage, pick and pack, order processing, shipping, returns management, and real-time inventory tracking. We integrate with major e-commerce platforms and can customize solutions based on your business needs.

Tracking & Delivery

How can I track my shipment?

You can track your shipment 24/7 through our website or mobile app using your tracking number. We also provide SMS and email updates at key milestones. For high-value shipments, we can provide GPS tracking links for real-time location monitoring.

What are your standard delivery times?

Local deliveries within the same city typically arrive within 24 hours. Intercity shipments usually take 2-3 business days depending on distance. Express services are available for faster delivery, and we can provide time-definite delivery windows for critical shipments.

What happens if no one is available to receive the delivery?

Our driver will attempt contact and leave a notice. We typically make 3 delivery attempts before returning the shipment to our facility. You can reschedule delivery, arrange for pickup at our warehouse, or authorize delivery to a neighbor or safe location.

Can I change the delivery address after shipment?

Address changes are possible before final delivery, though fees may apply depending on the new location and how far the shipment has progressed. Contact our customer service immediately with your tracking number to request an address change.

Do you provide proof of delivery?

Yes, we provide electronic proof of delivery including recipient signature (when applicable), time/date stamp, and sometimes photos. These are available in your online account and can be emailed upon request. Business clients can receive POD reports for multiple shipments.

What is your on-time delivery rate?

We maintain a 99% on-time delivery rate for standard shipments and a 100% rate for time-critical express shipments. Our operations team continuously monitors shipments to prevent and resolve any potential delays.

International Shipping

Which countries do you ship to internationally?

We provide international shipping to most countries worldwide through our global network of partners. Popular destinations include the UAE, Saudi Arabia, USA, UK, Canada, China, and European countries. Contact us for specific country availability and requirements.

What international shipping methods do you offer?

We offer air freight (fastest), sea freight (most economical), and land freight to neighboring countries. For time-sensitive shipments, we can arrange express air services. We'll recommend the best option based on your budget, timeline, and shipment characteristics.

Who handles customs clearance for international shipments?

Our international partners handle customs clearance at destination countries, working with licensed customs brokers. We'll guide you through all documentation requirements and help minimize duties when possible. For complex shipments, we can arrange pre-clearance before arrival.

What documents are required for international shipping?

Requirements vary by country but typically include commercial invoice, packing list, certificate of origin, and any special permits for restricted items. We provide complete checklists and templates to ensure your documentation meets all requirements.

How long does international shipping take?

Air freight typically takes 3-10 days door-to-door depending on destination. Sea freight takes 20-45 days. These estimates include transit time and customs clearance. Express air services can deliver in as little as 2-3 days to major global cities.

Can you help with import/export regulations?

Yes, our international shipping experts stay current on all import/export regulations and can advise on requirements, restrictions, duties, and documentation for both Pakistan and destination countries. We help ensure full compliance to prevent delays or issues.
