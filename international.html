Shipping Methods

We offer flexible shipping options to meet your budget and timeline requirements.

Ocean Freight

Best for: Large shipments, heavy cargo, cost-sensitive shipments

International Shipping FAQs

Answers to common questions about our international shipping services.

What documents are required for international shipping?

Required documents typically include commercial invoice, packing list, certificate of origin, bill of lading/airway bill, and any required permits or licenses. The exact documentation depends on your commodity, value, and destination country. We provide complete guidance on all documentation requirements for your specific shipment.

How are customs duties and taxes calculated?

Customs duties are calculated based on the harmonized tariff code of your goods, their declared value, and the trade agreements between origin and destination countries. Taxes (like VAT or GST) are calculated as a percentage of the goods value plus duties. We provide estimated duty calculations during quoting and handle all payments at destination.

What items are prohibited for international shipping?

Prohibited items vary by country but commonly include weapons, hazardous materials, perishable foods, certain electronics, and restricted pharmaceuticals. We maintain updated lists of prohibited and restricted items for all major destinations and will advise you during the booking process if any restrictions apply to your shipment.

How can I track my international shipment?

All international shipments receive a unique tracking number that can be monitored through our online tracking portal. You'll receive automatic email/SMS updates at key milestones including departure, arrival, customs clearance, and final delivery. For air freight, we provide real-time GPS tracking of your shipment.

What happens if my shipment is delayed at customs?

Our local customs brokers will immediately address any clearance issues. Common reasons for delays include missing/incomplete documentation or random inspections. We maintain pre-clearance arrangements in many countries to minimize delays and provide daily updates until your shipment is released.

Do you offer temperature-controlled international shipping?

Yes, we provide specialized temperature-controlled shipping solutions for pharmaceuticals, food products, and other sensitive goods. Options include refrigerated containers (reefers), insulated packaging, and climate-controlled air freight with 24/7 temperature monitoring and documentation.
