Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about contacting and working with Jiffy Logistics.

How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry?

We pride ourselves on quick response times. Phone calls are answered immediately during business hours, WhatsApp messages typically within 30 minutes, and emails within 4 hours. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our 24/7 support line at +92 301 3127332.

What information should I provide when requesting a quote?

For the most accurate quote, please provide: package dimensions and weight, pickup and delivery locations, any special handling requirements, and your preferred timeline. The more details you can provide, the more accurate our quote will be.

Do you offer emergency same-day delivery services?

Yes, we offer emergency same-day delivery services in most major cities. Availability depends on the time of your request and the specific locations. Please call us as early as possible to arrange same-day service.

Can I track my shipment after it's been dispatched?

Absolutely! All shipments come with real-time tracking. You'll receive a tracking number that you can use on our website or mobile app to monitor your shipment's progress from pickup to delivery.
