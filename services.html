Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our logistics services.

What areas do you serve for local deliveries?

We provide local delivery services in all major cities across Pakistan, including Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad, Rawalpindi, Faisalabad, Peshawar, and more. Our coverage is constantly expanding to serve more areas.

How can I track my shipment?

You can track your shipment using the tracking widget on our website or mobile app by entering your tracking number. You'll receive real-time updates via SMS and email as well.

What are your delivery timeframes?

Local deliveries typically arrive within 24 hours. National shipments usually take 2-3 business days depending on the destination. We also offer express options for faster delivery.

Do you offer insurance for shipments?

Yes, we offer comprehensive insurance options for all shipments. Basic coverage is included, and you can purchase additional protection for valuable items.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash on delivery, bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and mobile payments. Business clients can also set up monthly billing accounts.
