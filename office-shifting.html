Complete Office Relocation Solutions

Moving your office is a complex operation that requires careful planning and execution to minimize business disruption. Our professional office shifting services are designed to handle all aspects of your commercial move, from initial planning to final setup at your new location.

We specialize in moving businesses of all sizes, from small startups to large corporate offices. Our team understands the importance of maintaining business continuity during the transition, which is why we offer flexible scheduling options including after-hours and weekend moves.

With years of experience in office relocations across Pakistan, we've developed efficient systems for packing, transporting, and setting up office equipment, furniture, and sensitive documents. Our trained staff handles your office assets with the utmost care, ensuring everything arrives safely at your new location.

Office Moving FAQs

How far in advance should we plan our office move?

We recommend planning at least 8-12 weeks in advance for small to medium offices, and 3-6 months for larger corporate moves. This allows time for proper planning, employee communication, and coordination with building management at both locations.

Do you provide packing materials for office moves?

Yes, we supply all necessary packing materials including boxes, bubble wrap, packing paper, furniture pads, and specialized containers for IT equipment. These can be included in your moving package or provided as a separate service.

How do you handle sensitive IT equipment during the move?

Our IT moving specialists use anti-static packing materials, climate-controlled transportation when needed, and proper padding for all sensitive equipment. We document all connections before disassembly and provide comprehensive reconnection services at your new location.

Can you help with furniture layout planning at our new office?

Absolutely! We offer space planning services to help optimize your new office layout. Our team can work with your floor plans to create an efficient workspace design and ensure all furniture is placed according to your specifications.

What happens if something gets damaged during the move?

All our moves include basic insurance coverage, with options to upgrade for more valuable items. In the rare event of damage, we have a straightforward claims process and work with trusted repair specialists to resolve any issues quickly.

Do you provide storage solutions if there's a gap between move-out and move-in dates?

Yes, we offer secure, climate-controlled storage facilities for short or long-term needs. Our warehouses are monitored 24/7 and can accommodate everything from file boxes to office furniture and equipment.
