Our Journey

From humble beginnings to becoming one of Pakistan's leading logistics providers

Jiffy Logistics was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to provide reliable, efficient, and affordable logistics solutions to businesses across Pakistan. What started as a small operation with just two vehicles has grown into a nationwide network serving thousands of satisfied customers.

Over the past decade, we've continuously expanded our services and invested in technology to stay at the forefront of the logistics industry. Our commitment to excellence has earned us the trust of businesses ranging from small e-commerce stores to large multinational corporations.
