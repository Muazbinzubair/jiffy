Storage Services FAQs

Answers to common questions about our storage and warehousing services.

What items can I store in your facility?

We accept most household and business items including furniture, appliances, documents, and inventory. Prohibited items include hazardous materials, perishables, illegal substances, flammable items, live plants, and animals. For special items like artwork, antiques, or wine collections, we offer specialized storage solutions.

How do I access my stored items?

Access is available during business hours (8AM-8PM, 7 days a week) with proper identification. You can visit your storage unit in person, or we can arrange delivery of specific items to your location if needed. For business clients, we offer 24/7 access options with prior arrangement.

Are my items insured while in storage?

Basic insurance coverage is included up to a certain value, and additional coverage options are available for high-value items. We recommend discussing your specific insurance needs with our team to ensure adequate protection. Our insurance covers damage from fire, theft, and natural disasters.

What are the minimum and maximum storage periods?

We offer flexible storage from as little as one week to several years. There's no maximum storage period, and we can accommodate both short-term and long-term needs. Our pricing becomes more economical for longer storage durations, with significant discounts for commitments of 6 months or more.

Do you provide packing services for storage?

Yes, we offer professional packing services to prepare your items for storage. Our team uses high-quality packing materials and techniques to ensure maximum protection during the storage period. We can provide everything from boxes and bubble wrap to specialized packing for fragile items, artwork, and electronics.
