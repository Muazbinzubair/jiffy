Expert Furniture & Appliance Moving

Moving furniture and appliances requires specialized knowledge, proper equipment, and careful handling techniques. Our furniture moving service is designed to protect your valuable pieces throughout the entire moving process, from initial wrapping to final placement in your new location.

Whether you're moving a single piece of furniture or an entire household's worth of items, our team has the expertise to handle pieces of all sizes and materials. From delicate antiques to heavy modern furniture, bulky appliances to intricate decorative pieces, we ensure everything is moved safely and efficiently.

Our service includes professional disassembly and reassembly of furniture when needed, proper protection using high-quality materials, and careful handling by trained specialists. We also provide appliance disconnection and reconnection services for items like washing machines, refrigerators, and other household appliances.

Furniture Moving FAQs

Answers to common questions about our furniture moving services.

Do you disassemble and reassemble furniture?

Yes, we provide complete disassembly and reassembly services for furniture that requires it. Our team is experienced with all types of furniture and uses proper tools to ensure safe disassembly and accurate reassembly. We'll carefully label all parts and hardware for easy reassembly at your new location.

How do you protect furniture during the move?

We use professional-grade protective materials including furniture blankets, plastic wrap, corner guards, and custom padding. Each piece is individually wrapped and secured to prevent scratches, dents, and other damage. For particularly valuable or delicate items, we can provide custom crating services for maximum protection.

Can you move large appliances like refrigerators?

Absolutely! We specialize in moving large appliances including refrigerators, washing machines, dryers, and stoves. We handle disconnection, secure transport, and reconnection at your new location. For appliances requiring special handling (like gas line disconnection), we recommend using licensed professionals for those specific tasks.

Do you move antique or valuable furniture?

Yes, we have extensive experience moving antique and high-value furniture. We use extra protective measures and specialized handling techniques for delicate and valuable pieces. For particularly valuable antiques, we offer white glove service with additional insurance options to give you complete peace of mind.

What if my furniture doesn't fit through doorways?

Our experienced team can assess and solve access challenges. We may disassemble furniture, use alternative routes, or employ specialized techniques to safely navigate tight spaces. In rare cases where furniture cannot be moved intact, we'll discuss options with you before proceeding.

Do you provide packing materials?

Yes, we provide all necessary packing materials as part of our service. This includes furniture blankets, plastic wrap, bubble wrap, corner protectors, and specialized padding materials. There's no need for you to purchase or provide any packing materials - we come fully prepared.
